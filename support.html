Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. How is the game played?
    Five in Row (Gomoku) is played on a 15x15 grid. The goal is to be the first to connect 5 of your pieces in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • 2. What difficulty levels are available?
    The game offers three difficulty levels:
    • Easy: Perfect for beginners.
    • Medium: A fair challenge for intermediate players.
    • Hard: Expert-level AI that punishes mistakes.
  • 3. How does the game economy work?
    The game features a daily limit of 10 free games, which resets every day. You can remove this limit and enjoy unlimited play by purchasing a Premium subscription.
  • 4. What are the system requirements?
    Five in Row requires an iOS device running iOS 17.6 or later.
  • 5. Does the game require an internet connection?
    No. Five in Row works completely offline for the core gameplay. However, an internet connection is required to restore purchases or validate your Premium subscription.
  • 6. How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
    Subscriptions are managed directly through your Apple ID. Go to your device's Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions to view, cancel, or change your subscription plan.
